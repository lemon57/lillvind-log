This is the log of **Lillvind**, a Sunwind 26 family cruiser — a classic Nordic sailing yacht designed by Swedish naval architect Tord Sundén (of International Folkboat fame) and built in Finland by Oy Nykra Ab in Nykarleby. Hull number 127, most likely from the late 1970s.

![Lillvind under sail in the Öresund, summer 2025]({{ '/assets/images/lillvind_sailing_2025.jpg' | relative_url }})

## The Boat

| | |
|---|---|
| Model | Sunwind 26 "Family Cruiser" |
| LOA | 7.80 m |
| LWL | 6.40 m |
| Beam | 2.60 m |
| Draught | 1.50 m |
| Displacement | ~2.30 t |
| Sail area | 34 m² |
| Drive | Saildrive |

Fin keel with a skeg-hung rudder, light-wind performer, and surprisingly roomy below for her size — 4–5 berths, a small galley, and a separate head. Built as a boat that's easy to handle short-handed while still being comfortable for a family weekend.
