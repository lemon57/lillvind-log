Ivan and Roman came down to fit the sprayhood. By the end of the morning, it was on — properly on.

## The Sprayhood

Roman had washed it at the end of last season. After a winter sitting folded, it came out looking almost new — the fabric clean, the frame straight, no mildew. Good call doing it before storage.

## Fitting

Getting the sprayhood into place and properly seated is never as quick as it looks. We worked through the mounting points methodically, made sure the frame was seated correctly, and got everything secured.

At one point Ivan had a close encounter with a screwdriver — the kind that reminds you to pay attention in a confined space. An emergency patch sorted things out and all hands remained operational. What doesn't kill you makes you stronger.

## No Tea Break

We didn't manage to stop for tea. The work just kept going and before we knew it the job was done. One of those sessions.

## Weather

No rain. For an April day working on deck, that counts as good weather. We'll take it.

## Result

The sprayhood is on. It fits properly, it looks good, and it's reliable. That's one less thing to worry about for the season ahead.
