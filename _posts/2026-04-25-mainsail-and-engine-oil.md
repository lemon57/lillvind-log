A working day at the berth. Strong wind kept us off the open water, so we focused on the jobs we could do alongside.

![Lillvind at the berth — mainsail on, sprayhood tightened]({{ '/assets/images/mainsail-oil-change-2026-04-25.jpg' | relative_url }})

## Mainsail

The mainsail came out of storage and went up the mast. We hoisted it to check the track and the slides, then lowered it cleanly, flaked it down on the boom, and put the lazy bag and mainsail cover over the top. Tidy stow — ready to sail next time out.

## Engine Service

With the boat steady at the berth, it was a good moment to take care of the engine.

- **Engine oil** — drained and refilled with fresh oil to spec.
- **Oil filter** — replaced and torqued, no leaks on the first run.

Started her up afterwards and let her run for a few minutes. Pressure normal, no drips, all clean.

## Sprayhood

The sprayhood needed a small follow-up from last week's fitting. We tightened the mounting points and snugged up the frame. It sits properly now — no slack, no movement.

## Weather

The wind was strong all day. Going out on open water wasn't sensible, so we didn't try. The berth was sheltered enough to work on deck without things flying around, which is all we needed.

## Result

Mainsail on and covered, engine freshly serviced, sprayhood tight. A productive day even without leaving the harbour — and the boat is in better shape than when we arrived.
